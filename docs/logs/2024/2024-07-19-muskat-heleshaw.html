Some notes on the Muskat and Hele-Shaw problems

2024-07-19

The original Muskat problem is first introduced in 1. It models the flow of two incompressible, immiscible fluids moving in a porous medium. Here

  • incompressible: (mathematically) $\mathrm{div}\; v = 0$, where $v$ is the velocity vector field of each fluid at each point in the domain (divergence of the flow velocity is zero).
  • immiscible: not forming a homogeneous mixture when added together.

  1. Morris Muskat. Two fluid systems in porous media. The encroachment of water into an oil sand. In: Physics 5.9 (1934), pp. 250–264. ↩

  2. Thomas Alazard, Quoc-Hung Nguyen, Endpoint Sobolev theory for the Muskat equation, arxiv ↩

  3. Luis Caffarelli and Luis Silvestre, Regularity theory for fully nonlinear integro-differential equations arxiv ↩

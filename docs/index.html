Son Tu

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor at The Mathematics Department at Michigan State University working with Prof. Olga Turanova. Before that I was a Ph.D. student of Prof. Hung V. Tran at University of Wisconsin - Madison.

My dissertation is about Partial Differential Equations, particularly questions related to Hamilton-Jacobi equations, asymptotic properties of solutions in connection with optimal control theory.


My current projects concentrate on the nonlinear behavior of solutions to certain types of Hamilton–Jacobi equations and their applications to the free boundary problem in some models of fluid dynamics.
